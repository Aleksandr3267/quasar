WHAT IS QUASAR?

WE ARE BUILDING A NEW FINANCIAL SYSTEM

Quasar is a decentralized reserve currency that is not tied to any fiat currency. Token $QUAS aims to curb inflation and preserve purchasing power for users.

HELP FOR USERS

Instead of spending their LP tokens to receive farming rewards, users can exchange their LP tokens for protocol management tokens at a reduced rate. This is done through a process called Bonding. Since the protocol never sells these LP tokens, liquidity is effectively locked in its treasury.

HELP TO PROTOCOLS

Quasar is a new platform to help protocols acquire their own liquidity. Protocols no longer have to pay out high incentives for renting liquidity, and also guarantee consistency of liquidity to facilitate transactions.

Quasar DAO is changing the DeFi world

The Quasar protocol is designed to allow the value of the $QUAS token to grow over time. This creates a hedge against inflation. Providing the $QUAS token with a pool of stablecoins ensures the stability of the token price.

Quasar rewards all members with cumulative interest by increasing their $QUAS token supply. All $QUAS minted for betting fees are backed by Quasar treasury reserves.

01

The Quasar DAO protocol owns almost all of its liquidity, which helps maintain price stability and treasury income. With the protocol's liquidity, Quasar is protected from adverse market conditions.

02
