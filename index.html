WHAT IS QUASAR?

WE ARE BUILDING A NEW FINANCIAL SYSTEM

Quasar is a community driven decentralized autonomous economic institution (DAEI) in the DeFi 2.0. It’s opens a new opportunities for the industry

HELP FOR USERS

Instead of spending their LP tokens to receive farming rewards, users can exchange their LP tokens for protocol management tokens at a reduced rate. This is done through a process called Bonding. Since the protocol never sells these LP tokens, liquidity is effectively locked in its treasury.

HELP TO PROTOCOLS

Quasar is a new platform to help protocols acquire their own liquidity. Protocols no longer have to pay out high incentives for renting liquidity, and also guarantee consistency of liquidity to facilitate transactions.
